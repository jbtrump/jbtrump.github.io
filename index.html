Research Experience

2020-Present: Research Assistant - Brigham Young University, Provo, Utah

I am currently conducting research at BYU about current practices for teaching introductory astronomy using a planetarium. As part of this research project, I created a survey that will be sent out to every planetarium in an educational instituion around the entire United States.

2018-2019: Research Assistant - Orson Pratt Observatory, Provo, Utah

I worked for about a year at the Orson Pratt Observatory on BYU campus aquiring and analyzing data on variable stars. Variable stars are stars that change in brightness over consistent intervals.

Summer 2017: Observer and Analyst, West Mountain Obs., Santaquin, Utah

I worked for a summer with other students at the West Mountain Observatory operated by BYU. It was a fun adventure that allowed me to truly experience life as an astronomer (being up all night and alseep during most of the day). I enjoyed the experience and learned a lot about telescope operation, and how to aquire quality data. See me next to the big telescope here.
